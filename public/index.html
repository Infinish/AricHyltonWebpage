About Me

My name is Aric Hylton I have been working with computers my entire life. One day me and my brother decided to make a mobile app called Balloon Quest. The process of creating and developing the game from start to finish has made me realize how much I enjoyed coding. Since the games release, becoming a better developer has been my main focus.

My main experience is with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and Node.js. I'm constantly keeping up to date with new documentation and enhancing my knowledge of current programming concepts.

A few of my other hobbies are powerlifting, rock-climbing, and pretty much anything outdoors. I like seeing a goal, mapping out the process of getting to the end, and then tackling each problem along the way.
